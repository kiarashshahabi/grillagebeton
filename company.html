Grillage Beton & Associates

Founded in March 1978 (Co. Reg. No. 31488), Grillage Beton & Associates (GBA) was an Iranian civil engineering and construction company active in reinforced-concrete buildings, urban infrastructure, drainage systems, and flood-control tunnels.

Under the founding and management of Eng. Jamshid Shahabi, GBA delivered public-service projects across Tehran and other regions of Iran for more than three decades.
