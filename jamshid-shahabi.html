Biography

Eng. Jamshid Shahabi was born in Tehran on 1 March 1948 (11.12.1326) to Fatemeh and Mohammad Ali Shahabi—both originally from Shahmirzad, Semnan—and grew up in a family of five siblings. He entered the University of Tehran in 1966 (1345) to study Civil Engineering, laying the academic foundation for a lifelong career in construction and public infrastructure.

In 1978, he founded Grillage Beton & Associates, through which he led and supervised reinforced-concrete building projects, urban infrastructure works, and major drainage and flood-control systems across Iran. His work was characterized by precision, responsibility, and a deep sense of public service.

Jamshid Shahabi married Minoosh in 1976 (1355). He was the devoted father of Kiarash (born 1977 / 1356) and Golnoosh (born 1980 / 1359), and later became a proud grandfather in 2014.

He passed away on 15 December 2022 (25.9.1401).

Legacy

His legacy lives on through the structures and civil works completed under his leadership—built to endure and to serve communities. The company project archive is available on the companion page.
